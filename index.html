The Cowfish brings to the world the first restaurant focusing on offering both the best sushi AND the best burger in town. Fresh, innovative, and delicious, the sushi segment of the menu is a unique balance of traditional and unconventional. On the other side, all natural, creative and hearty, the burgers entice with fresh ingredients and bold flavors.The Cowfish is a come one, come all operation. It has an atmosphere to suit all walks of life.

The Cowfish Sushi Burger Bar is located in the Village at SouthPark, next to the mall.
